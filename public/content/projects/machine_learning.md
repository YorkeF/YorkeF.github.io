# Machine Learning — Tomato Ripeness Classifier
## Replication of published AI research
**Period:** August 2024 – December 2024
**Status:** Completed
**Source:** github.com/YorkeF/Machine-Learning

---

## Overview

Worked in a group to replicate the results of a published study using
AI to classify whether a tomato was ripe. The goal was to reproduce
the paper's methodology and validate its findings using our own
training pipeline.

## Contributions

- Managed dataset pre-processing: cleaning, augmentation, and
  train/validation/test splits
- Ran model training using the architecture referenced in the paper
- Assisted in debugging and iterating on hyperparameters to improve
  classification accuracy

`Python` `PyTorch` `Google Colab`
